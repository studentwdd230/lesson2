The Weather Conditions

Purpose

Weather conditions warrnings are important forecasts because they are used to protect life and property. It is important to determine how we stay warm or cool enough to survive and how to stay comfortable in those times like to know what type of clothing we wear, what foods to grow and eat and what resources do we have. It can greatly influence on the success and safety of any outdoor activity. Weather conditions determine what we wear, how far and where we can venture, what equipment we'll need and how long we can safely brave the elements.

Audience

Most weather forecasts are targeted to one of four areas. These include the private sector, agriculture, the generalpublic. and other meteorologists.
